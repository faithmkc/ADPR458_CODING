About Us

We are a locally owned company looking to help you improve your health the natural and safe way. Our Doctor, Jason McCullough, has made it his life mission to help as many people as possible.

How We Got Here

We originated just across the street as a small business, but we recently upgraded to new facilities and now offer online help to reach even more people!

Services We Offer

We offer a broad spectrum of services to improve your health. We start with X-rays to get a good look at your bones and understand where we need to make some adjustments, literally. From there, we can do color therapy, mobility exercises, red laser therapy, and much more!

About Doctor Jay

He was originally born in Washington D.C. and grew up in Allentown, Pennsylvania. He attended the University of Miami and the University of Nebraska, playing football at both. Later, he found his love for chiropractic after he received his first-ever adjustment and then proceeded to get his doctorate at Palmer Chiropractic in Iowa!
